The Consultant Behind Every Project

Don Dukes is not your average concrete contractor. He's a consultant who brings manufacturer-level knowledge, industry connections, and 26 years of El Paso experience directly to your project.

As a past president of the El Paso International Chapter of the American Concrete Institute (ACI), Don has spent his career at the intersection of production, engineering, and application. His early years as a concrete material and equipment supplier gave him rare access — to the chemists who formulate repair products, the engineers who specify them, and the contractors who put them to use.

That foundation is what sets Don Dukes Concrete apart. Every service Don performs is grounded in hands-on training from the manufacturers themselves — the people who develop and test the products in real conditions. He has worked alongside architects, engineers, and craftsmen to ensure that what gets installed actually performs.

Over 20 years in selling concrete forming, shoring, scaffolding, and concrete accessories gave Don a systems-level view of construction that most repair contractors never develop. When you bring Don in, you're getting a problem-solver with a full tool belt — both technical and practical.

"Each of the services I perform, I have been given the knowledge of products and equipment during class, with hands-on training, from those that develop and manufacture performance products." — Don Dukes
